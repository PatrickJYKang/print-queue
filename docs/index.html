Print Queue Bot

A fair, automated Discord queue for shared 3D printers

What is this?

Print Queue Bot helps communities share 3D printers fairly. Users join a single queue in Discord and are notified when it’s their turn to claim a printer. The system relies on user‑reported times and predictions to schedule notifications. No hardware integrations are required.

How to use

  • Run /join job_name est_time to join with your estimated print time.
  • When notified, run /claim printer to reserve a printer.
  • At the printer:
    • If the previous print is still running: /notdone printer remaining_time (releases your claim and reschedules your turn).
    • If the previous print has ended: /close printer success|fail [notes] [photo] (photo required for fail).
    • Then start your job: /report started printer.
  • Use /status and /myjobs to check queue and your jobs.

Backend

  • ETAs and printer availability are based on your self‑reported durations.
  • A scheduler predicts when a printer will be free and pings the next user.
  • Access hours are respected: if a finish happens outside access hours, claim/start timers begin next open period.
  • No hardware polling or MQTT; all transitions are user‑reported via commands.
  • Fairness is enforced by configurable claim and start windows.

Commands at a glance

  • /join add your job with an estimate
  • /claim reserve a specific printer
  • /notdone report previous print not finished and remaining time (releases your claim)
  • /close close the previous job (success or fail; photo required for fail)
  • /report started confirm you started your print
  • /status, /myjobs, /update_time, /cancel
